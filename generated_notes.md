# Doctor Who Mod - Update Notes

## What Changed in This Iteration

- **Crafting Recipes Added**: Portal blocks (Gallifrey Portal, TARDIS, and 8 planetary portals) can now be crafted using diamonds, gold, emeralds, ender pearls, and nether stars
- **New Entity Drops**: Daleks drop Dalek Circuitry and Dalek Plungers; Cybermen drop Cyberman Heads and Cyber Circuitry; Weeping Angels drop Angel Stone Fragments; Sontarans drop Sontaran Helmets; Time Lords drop Time Lord Robes and Sonic Screwdrivers
- **TARDIS Block**: Added the iconic TARDIS police box block that teleports players to Gallifrey when used or walked through
- **8 New Planets**: Gallifrey (Time Lords), Skaro (Daleks), Cybus (Cybermen parallel Earth), Mondas (Cybermen origin), Clom, Ravenous, Midnight, Trenzalore (battlefield), and Omega's volcanic domain
- **5 New Alien Races**: Added Cybermen, Weeping Angels, Sontarans, and Time Lords as new hostile and friendly mobs
- **Sonic Screwdriver Item**: Craftable tool that can interact with doors, buttons, and levers
- **Planetary Keys**: 8 craftable keys (Skaro, Cybus, Mondas, Clom, Ravenous, Midnight, Trenzalore, Omega) to teleport between dimensions
- **Sound Effects**: Configured custom sounds for TARDIS travel, Exterminate screams, Sonic Screwdriver activation, and each alien race

## Overview

This mod brings the Doctor Who universe to Minecraft with multiple planets, iconic creatures, and travel mechanics. Explore the universe using TARDIS portals, planetary keys, or craft the Sonic Screwdriver to interact with the world.

## Features

### Dimensions & Planets
- **Gallifrey**: Home of the Time Lords with unique orange skies
- **Skaro**: Wasteland home of the Daleks, dark and desolate
- **Cybus**: Parallel Earth ruled by Cybermen
- **Mondas**: Origin planet of Cybermen
- **Trenzalore**: A battlefield dimension with all alien races
- **Omega's Domain**: Volcanic realm of the legendary Omega

### Mobs
- **Dalek**: Hostile pepper-pot enemy that shoots extermination beams
- **Cyberman**: Armored undead that upgrades victims
- **Weeping Angel**: Creepy statue that only moves when not observed
- **Sontaran**: Stocky warrior race with powerful attacks
- **Time Lord**: Friendly NPC that can provide guidance

### Items & Tools
- **Sonic Screwdriver**: Opens doors and interacts with redstone
- **Planetary Keys**: One for each dimension (8 total)
- **TARDIS**: The iconic blue police box for dimension travel
- **Crafting Materials**: Dalek/Cyberman parts from entity drops

## How to Use

1. **Craft a Gallifrey Portal**: Use diamonds, gold blocks, emeralds, and ender pearls
2. **Craft the TARDIS**: Use diamonds, gold, and a Gallifrey Portal
3. **Travel to Dimensions**: Use the TARDIS block or planetary keys
4. **Fight Enemies**: Kill Daleks, Cybermen, and other aliens for rare drops
5. **Meet Time Lords**: Find friendly Time Lords in Gallifrey

## Tips & Tricks

- Weeping Angels only move when you're NOT looking at them
- Sonic Screwdriver works on doors, buttons, and levers
- Planetary keys get slightly damaged with each use
- Each planet has its own unique hostile mob spawns
- Time Lords can be a peaceful source of Sonic Screwdrivers

## Known Limitations

- Custom textures require external asset packs (placeholder textures used)
- Some dimensions may not generate terrain until explored
- Sound effects require adding actual .ogg files to assets folder
